Team Vayuvega

Vayuvega is the Aerospace Division of Team Vikasana who arededicated to exploring the possibilities of atmospheric research by developing CANSATs. Team Vikasana is the student research and innovation team that is working under the guidance of professors at the Research and Innovation dept, Presidency University, Bengaluru. We are a team of students that aim to conceptualize, build and deliver top-notch robots. Our team is made up of passionate students from diverse academic backgrounds who share a common goal of pushing the boundaries of what's possible.
