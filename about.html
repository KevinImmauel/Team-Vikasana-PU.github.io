Vikasana

Team Vikasana is the official student research and innovation team of Presidency University, Bengaluru, working under the guidance of professors at the Research and Innovation dept, Presidency University, Bengaluru. We are a team of students that aim to conceptualize, build and deliver top-notch robots.

Vikasana builds Technologies and innovations to contribute to a better learning experience in the university and represent and participate in technical conventions, conferences and competitions hosted in the collegiate, state levels in India and abroad.

We are always in pursuit to expand our apprenticeship in research, innovation, robots, space and other areas of engineering and technology.

Vision

To appreciate,encourage and be patrons of research and innovation and excel whilst leaving our mark in the field of science and technology in all our endeavours.

Mission

To represent and participate as the official ‘University Robotics Team’ in technical conventions, conferences and competitions hosted in the collegiate, state levels in India and abroad.

To host international, national, intercollegiate and intra-collegiate conventions, conferences and competitions on behalf of the University in partnership with the technical industry.

To conceptualise, build and deliver top notch robots, technologies and AI machines to contribute to a better learning experience in the university.

To foster technical apprenticeship and an innovative spirit amongst our fellow students and the university.
